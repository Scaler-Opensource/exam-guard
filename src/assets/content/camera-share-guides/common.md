## **Step 1: Check the Camera LED Indicator**  
**Is the camera light ON, but the camera is not visible?**  
![enter image description here](https://d2beiqkhq929f0.cloudfront.net/public_assets/assets/000/114/132/original/camera_light_indicator.webp?1742211910)
  - ✅ **Yes** → [Go to Step 2](#step-2-close-other-apps-using-the-camera)  
  - ❌ **No** → [Go to Step 3](#step-3-check-browser-camera-permissions)  

---

## **Step 2: Close Other Apps Using the Camera**  
🧐 **Check for background apps with camera access: Follow these steps -**  
- **Windows:** `Start > Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera`  
- **Mac:** `Apple Menu > System Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera`  

- 🔄 **Close all such apps** and restart your browser and try again

📵 **Disable phone-based camera apps** that may interfere:  
   - Samsung Flow, Dell Mobile Connect, Apple Continuity Camera etc may interfere with your camera feed
   - Disconnect phone from **Bluetooth or USB tethering** before starting the test.  

---

## **Step 3: Check Browser Camera Permissions**  
🔍 Ensure camera access is **allowed** for your browser:  

💻 **For Chrome (Windows & Mac)**  

  ![enter image description here](https://d2beiqkhq929f0.cloudfront.net/public_assets/assets/000/114/129/original/site_camera_access.png?1742211240)
  - Click the **camera icon** in the address bar → **Site settings** → Set **Camera** to **Allow**.  
  - Under **Default Behavior,** select **Sites can ask to use your camera**.  
  - Disable **Ask before accessing**
  - If present, under **Allowed to use your camera,** next to `https://proctoring.scaler/`, click **Delete**.  
  - Refresh the Scaler proctoring page and grant camera access when prompted.  

💻 **For Safari (Mac)** 

![enter image description here](https://d2beiqkhq929f0.cloudfront.net/public_assets/assets/000/114/874/original/image_%286%29.png?1742373881) 
  - `Safari > Settings > Websites > Camera` → Ensure the website has access.  
  - Choose **Apple Menu > System Settings > Privacy & Security**.  
  - Click **Camera** and ensure access is turned **ON** for the browser.  
  - If **Camera** is not visible, upgrade to macOS Mojave or later.  
  - The camera turns on automatically when used by an app, indicated by a green light.  

- **ForFirefox**  
  - Refer to [this article](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/how-manage-your-camera-and-microphone-permissions#:~:text=If%20you've%20turned%20off,submenu%20for%20the%20Firefox%20app) for detailed steps.  

🔄 **Refresh the page** after adjusting settings.

## **Step 4: Verify System-Level Camera Permissions**  
🖥️ **Windows:**  

![enter image description here](https://d2beiqkhq929f0.cloudfront.net/public_assets/assets/000/114/130/original/camera_app.png?1742211475)
  - `Start > Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera` (Windows 11)  
  - Ensure **Camera access for this device** and **Let apps access your camera** are **ON**.  

💻 **Mac:**  

![enter image description here](https://d2beiqkhq929f0.cloudfront.net/public_assets/assets/000/114/875/original/image_%287%29.png?1742373923)
  - `Apple Menu > System Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera`  
  - Ensure the checkbox next to your browser is **enabled**.  
  - The list shows apps that have requested camera access; ensure the browser is checked.  

⚙️ If using non-Microsoft Store apps, ensure **desktop app access is enabled**.  

---

## **Step 5: Check for Camera Block Shortcuts**  
⌨️ **Physical Camera Switch:** Some laptops have a physical toggle/button for the webcam. Ensure it is **ON**. (Below image is an example of the same)
![enter image description here](https://d2beiqkhq929f0.cloudfront.net/public_assets/assets/000/114/131/original/camera_switch.png?1742211762)
⌨️ **Function Key Lock:** Press `Fn + Camera (F8/F10)` to toggle camera access.  
⚙️ **BIOS Settings:** Restart your laptop → Enter **BIOS settings** → Check for **camera-related options**.

## **Step 6: Test Camera Preview**  
🔄 **Reload the Scaler proctoring page**.  
📷 **Test the camera using:**  
  - **Windows:** Open the **Camera** app.  
  - **Mac:** Use **FaceTime** or **Photo Booth**.  

---

## **Step 7: Additional Troubleshooting**  
🔄 **Update camera drivers** via `Device Manager` (Windows) or `Software Update` (Mac).  
🛠️ **Run the Windows Camera Troubleshooter** to detect and fix common issues.  
🔐 **Check antivirus/firewall settings** that may be blocking camera access.
